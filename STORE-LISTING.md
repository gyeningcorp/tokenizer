## Full Description

**Know exactly what every message costs — before you hit send.**

Tokenizer is the only browser extension that shows you live token counts, dollar costs, CO₂ emissions, and energy usage for every prompt you type — across 30+ AI platforms simultaneously.

---

### ⚡ What It Does

As you type, Tokenizer shows you:
- **Token count** — updates live with every keystroke
- **Input & output costs** — in dollars, per message and per session
- **Cross-model comparison** — see what the same prompt costs on GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA, DeepSeek, and Mistral side by side
- **Token breakdown** — every word color-coded by token cost (green=1 token, red=4+ tokens), with syllable analysis
- **Data center energy** — estimated Wh/mWh used + CO₂ emitted per prompt (based on IEA 2024 AI Energy Report)
- **Session totals** — cumulative tokens, cost, and energy for your entire session
