🚀 We just launched Tokenizer — a free, open-source browser extension that shows you exactly what your AI prompts cost in real time.

Every time you type a message into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, or 30+ other AI platforms — Tokenizer shows you:

🔢 **Live token count** (updates with every keystroke)
💰 **Dollar cost** (input + output, per message and per session)
📊 **Cross-model comparison** — same prompt, 6 models, side by side
🔤 **Word-by-word token breakdown** — color-coded so you can see which words "cost" the most
⚡ **Data center energy** — estimated Wh used + CO₂ emitted per prompt

Everything runs 100% locally in your browser. Zero data collection. No account needed. MIT licensed.

Supports: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Mistral, Copilot, Meta AI, Perplexity, Cursor, Bolt.new, Ollama, LM Studio + more.
